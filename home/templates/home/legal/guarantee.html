Le Patissier abides by the 'Money Back' guarantee specified in the quality charter of the French Association of Distance Selling Companies (FEVAD).

In case of a faulty item, quality problem, preparation or packaging issue or any damaged item during transport (you must notify Le Patissier within 14 days after reception), we will send you a pre-paid shipping label to return the items (metropolitan France only).
